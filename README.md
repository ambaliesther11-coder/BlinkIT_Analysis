# BlinkIT sales Analysis

### OVERVIEW

This Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard is a business intelligence project developed to analyze sales performance, customer preferences, and outlet operations across different outlet types, sizes, and locations in India from 2011 to 2022.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key business metrics such as total sales, average sales, product performance, customer ratings, and outlet distribution. 

The goal of the project is to transform raw sales data into actionable insights that can support strategic business decisions and improve operational efficiency.


### OBJECTIVE
To conduct a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's:
- sales performance,
- customer satisfaction, and
- inventory distribution to
- identify key insights and opportunities
- for optimization using various KPIs and visualizations in Power BI and Excel
- To provide an interactive dashboard for easy visualization and interpretation of business data.

### INSIGHTS
1. Sales Performance
- The business generated a total sales value of approximately ₹1.2 million.
- Average sales per item were about ₹141.
- A total of 1,559 items were analyzed.
- The average customer rating was approximately 3.9.

2. Products insights
- Low-fat products generated higher total sales compared to regular-fat products.
- Fruits & Vegetables contributed the highest sales among all product categories.
- Snack Foods and Household products were also among the top-performing categories.
- Seafood and Breakfast items recorded relatively lower sales performance.

3. Outlet Insights
- Tier 3 outlets recorded the highest sales compared to Tier 1 and Tier 2 locations.
- Medium-sized outlets contributed the largest percentage of total sales.
- Supermarket Type 1 outperformed other outlet types in sales and item count.
- Older outlets did not always generate higher revenue, indicating that outlet age alone does not determine performance.

4. Customer Insights
- Customer ratings remained relatively stable across product categories.
- Both low-fat and regular-fat products maintained similar customer satisfaction levels.

### INFERENCES
- Product category significantly influences total sales performance.
- Consumer demand appears stronger for healthier or low-fat product options.
- Geographic location plays a major role in sales generation.
- Medium-sized outlets may offer the best balance between operational cost and customer reach.
- Certain outlet types consistently outperform others, suggesting stronger operational strategies or customer engagement.


### CONCLUSION 
The Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard successfully provides meaningful insights into sales trends, customer preferences, and outlet performance from 2011 to 2022. The project demonstrates how business intelligence tools such as Power BI and Excel can convert raw data into interactive and actionable insights for decision-making.

The analysis revealed that outlet location, product category, and outlet size strongly influence revenue generation. Additionally, low-fat products and medium-sized outlets emerged as strong contributors to overall sales performance.

Overall, the dashboard serves as an effective analytical tool that can help management identify opportunities, improve operational efficiency, and support strategic business growth.



### RECOMMENDATIONS
- Increase investment and inventory in high-performing categories such as Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods.
- Expand healthy and low-fat product offerings due to their strong market performance.
- Focus expansion efforts on Tier 3 locations where sales performance is strongest.
- Optimize medium-sized outlet operations since they contribute significantly to revenue.
- Improve underperforming product categories through promotions and customer feedback analysis.
- Maintain product quality and customer satisfaction to sustain ratings.
- Incorporate time-series analysis for yearly growth tracking.
